    Why this initiative?

    We work for different projects over time and build automation frameworks involving different tools/tech-stack. A library of such frameworks will be a great place to learn, utilize, share and evolve our knowledge as a community. This is the visioning behind this open-source initiative by ThoughtWorks QA Community.

    The Basics

    Every repo has few sample scenarios in executable state. This is ensured by integration with Travis (a hosted continuous integration service). Secondly, we are working towards self-contained repos, meaning every repo will encompass its test code, dependencies managed, and application-under-testing included.

    Like to join the community?

    Checkout the repository you are interested on and utilize it to learn or jump-start your project framework. After all, its the usage which justifies any effort.

    Share the framework you are working on. This will enrich our library and grow our expertise as a community.

    Report issues or observations you come across, which will help in evolving existing frameworks.
